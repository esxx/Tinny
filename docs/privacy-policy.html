Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

App Name: Tinny

Platform: iOS

Important: Tinny does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information or usage data. The app operates entirely on your device.

1. No Data Collection

Tinny does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information or usage data. The app operates entirely on your device with no external servers or cloud services.

2. Local-Only Operation

All app functionality works locally on your iOS device:

  • Notification scheduling and delivery
  • Sound playback
  • User preferences storage
  • Purchase verification (handled by Apple's secure system)

3. Notifications

The app uses iOS local notifications to deliver hourly chimes. These notifications are scheduled and delivered entirely by your device's operating system. No notification data leaves your device.

4. In-App Purchases

If you choose to purchase Tinny Premium:

  • All transactions are processed by Apple through the App Store
  • We do not have access to your payment information
  • Purchase verification is handled securely by Apple's system
  • No purchase history is stored on our servers

5. No Third-Party Services

Tinny does not integrate with any third-party services, analytics platforms, advertising networks, or social media platforms.

6. Device Permissions

The app requests only the permissions necessary for functionality:

  • Notifications: To deliver hourly chime alerts
  • No other permissions are requested or required

7. Children's Privacy

Tinny does not collect any data from anyone, including children. The app is suitable for all ages as it contains no data collection mechanisms.

8. Changes to This Policy

If we make changes to this privacy policy in the future, we will update this page. Since no data is collected, policy changes would only reflect additional transparency about our non-collection practices.

9. Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please visit our Support page.
